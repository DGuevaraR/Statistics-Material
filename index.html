Interpretation: The red shaded region represents the Type I error probability, which is the probability of rejecting H₀ when H₀ is true. The blue shaded region represents the Type II error probability, which is the probability of failing to reject H₀ when H₁ is true. The power of the test is 1 − β, where β is the Type II error probability.
